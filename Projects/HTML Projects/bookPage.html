Chiranjeevi
Padmabhushan Dr. Chiranjeevi is the most popular film actor turned politician in Andhra Pradesh. His fans call him Megastar and Annayya. He was born on August 22, 1955 at Narasapur, in AndhraPradesh. He made his debut into Tollywood with the movie Punadirallu . He became No.1 celebrity in AndharaPradesh.
People like him very much because he does everything sincerly. Young people adore him because he inspires them with his words and voice. Elders respect him very much because he sincerely does social service. His co artists support him a lot as he honestly encourages them. People love him a lot because he does maintain punctuality, sincerity and discipline. People believe him more than anything because he keeps his word. He lives in the hearts of the people forever as he honestly shows his concern towards the poor and needy. He does challenge the situations because he strongly believes himself. He doesn't hurt anybody with his words and voice, as he knows the value of them. He does make miracles happen with the love and affection that he gets from across the world.
He started his career in a humble way and became the megastar and ruled the Telugu film industry fro over a decade. He became a role model to many heroes with his splendid acting and sincere behaviour. He did learn a lesson from every failure and became successful person in his personal, professional and social life. He started Chiranjeevi blood and eye bank on October2, 1998 through which he saved a good number of lives. He voluntarily participated in various social service activities and thus motivated the youth in particular. These activities earned him a lot of name and afame across the globe. He did win the prestigious award Padmabhushan for his outstanding contribution in his personal and professional life. He directly entered into the lives of the people by launching a political party Praja Rajyam on August 26, 2008 at Tirupathi.
